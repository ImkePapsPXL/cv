Why programming?

Why the education programming?

I love coding. I started an evening education while I was still working on webdesign and grafics design. Here I noticed how much I like to create. Creativity, curiosity and creating something new has always been inside me. I did not find fullness and satisfaction in my jobs so far, but the evening education did. This is how I figured out what I want to do and what I enjoy. So I decided to pursue this. of course the previous jobs are no loss, I learned a lot of hard and soft skills and learned what I need in a job. I know for a fact I like having good colleagues u can work with, but also being independent. Clear expectations is something I need, other than that I can make my own plans and work independently. Good and honest communication is very important to me.

Why I believe this job is right for me?

As I said before, I love creating. I am independent enough to solve problems myself and I enjoy finding solutions to problems and make a beautifull result that makes people happy. I can go hours working on code. Once I am focussed it is hard to break and I keep going until I am satisfied. Seeing the program or website change and do what u like after some hard work puts a smile on my face. With this kind of job my creativity can be let loose on the job. This is something I desperately missed in the past jobs.
